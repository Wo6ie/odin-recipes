Lasagne Recipe

Description

Insert description here

Ingredients

  • Mince: Lean Irish Beef [400g] or Vegan Mince [400g].
  • Onion: Red or White [1 medium sized].
  • Chopped Tomatoes: 1 tin [or 150g fresh].
  • Bolognese Sauce: 1 Jar [400g].
  • Cheese Sauce: 1 Jar[400g]
  • Herbs: Parsley [2tbsp] & Garlic [1 clove, crushed].
  • Sugar [1tbsp] [neutralise tomato acidity].
  • Pasta Sheets: Fresh or dried [white or wholewheat].
  • Cheese: Parmesan, Cheddar or Dairy Free [cottage cheese optional].
  • Eggs: Optional [alternative for jar of cheese sauce].

Steps

  • Cook Mince:
    • Heat dash of olive oil in medium sized pan.
    • Add mince and cook until brown drain excess fat using lid and towel.
    • Add diced onion and continue cooking until onions are soft.
    • Add sauces, chopped tomatoes & bolognese sauce.
    • Add herbs: half parsley, garlic and basil [put aside some basil for garnish topping at end].
    • Add sugar.
    • Optional: Add dash of salt & oregano.

  • Cook Pasta Sheets:
    • Bring medium sized pan of water to the boil.
    • Add dash of salt.
    • Add pasta sheets.
    • Boil until soft use fork to test.

  • Make Cheese Layer:
    • Grate cottage cheese, parmesan cheese [or dairy free]in a bowl.
    • Add jar of cheese sauce to bowl of cheese and mix.
    • Optional: mix two eggs and add to cheese [homemade cheese sauce].

  • Assemble Lasagna:
    • Step 1: Add 1 inch base mince layer to pasta dish.
    • Step 2: Add a layer of cheese sauce [with cheese already mixed in].
    • Repeat: Step 1, then Step 2, until pan of mince empty.
    • Top Layer: Add remaining cheese sauce to the top of lasagne.

  • Bake Lasagna:
    • Pre-heat Oven: 200°C for 10 minutes.
    • Cover: Lasagna dish with kitchen foil.
    • Bake: Lasagna in oven for 30 minutes [set timer].
    • Brown: Remove Lasagna from oven, remove foil and cook until golden brown.
    • Optional: Once foil removed, add another layer of grated cheese & basil to garnish.
